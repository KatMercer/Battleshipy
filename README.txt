A Classic Game of Battleship

Attempt to destroy your opponent's fleet before they destroy yours!

0-2 Players (2 Computers, 1 Player & 1 Computer, 2 Players)

Ships are as follow:
A = Aircraft Carrier (5 tiles long)
B = Battleship (4 tiles long)
c = Cruiser (3 tiles long)
S = Submarine (3 tiles long)
D = Destroyer (2 tiles long)

On your board, a ship that has been hit will display a lowercase letter on the section with damage, and a tile that has been missed will display '.'
On your radar board (representing your opponent's hidden board), a hit will display as 'x' and a miss will display as 'o'
